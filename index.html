Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

2 Players  ·  Local

Meta
  • The board is a 3×3 grid of small Tic Tac Toe boards.
  • Where you play within a small board tells your opponent which small board they must play in next.
  • Example: play in the top-right cell of any small board → opponent must play in the top-right small board.
  • If the target small board is already won or full, the opponent may play in any open board.
  • Win three small boards in a row (row, column, or diagonal) to win the game.
  • The highlighted board(s) show where the current player must move.
